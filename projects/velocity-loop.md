<section id="project-description">
  <h2>About the Project</h2>
  <p>
    Velocity Loop is a third-person endless runner built in Unreal Engine. Players sprint through 
    procedurally generated environments, collecting coins and dodging obstacles as the speed 
    continuously ramps up.
  </p>
  <p>
    The game focuses on tight responsive movement, escalating difficulty, and clean visual feedback 
    to keep players in a flow state.
  </p>
</section>

<section id="project-tech">
  <h2>Tech Breakdown</h2>
  <div class="tech-grid">
    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Engine</h3>
      <p>Unreal Engine 5 — Blueprints for gameplay logic and level construction.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Procedural Generation</h3>
      <p>Tile based procedural level system that dynamically spawns and despawns track sections, obstacles, coins, and powerups as the player runs.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>3rd Person Movement</h3>
      <p>Custom character movement with lane switching, jump, and slide mechanics.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Difficulty Scaling</h3>
      <p>Speed scales over time using for faster player reaction times.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Coin & Score System</h3>
      <p>Real-time score tracking with coin multipliers and high score persistence.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Version Control</h3>
      <p>Git with LFS for asset management across the project lifecycle.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
