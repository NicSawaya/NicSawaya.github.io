<section id="project-description">
  <h2>About the Project</h2>
  <p>
    Orbit Protocol is a roguelite FPS set aboard a decaying 1970s space colony. 
    Players fight through procedurally influenced combat arenas, unlocking modular 
    weapon systems and abilities as the colony falls apart around them.
  </p>
  <p>
    The game focuses on tight moment-to-moment feel, responsive FPS mechanics, 
    and a strong retro-futuristic visual identity inspired by 1970s sci-fi aesthetics.
  </p>
</section>

<section id="project-tech">
  <h2>Tech Breakdown</h2>

  <div class="tech-grid">

    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Engine</h3>
      <p>Unreal Engine 5 — core gameplay systems, blueprint prototyping, and final C++ implementation.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>FPS Mechanics</h3>
      <p>Custom character movement, weapon handling, and hit detection built in C++ with Blueprint exposure for rapid iteration.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Weapon Systems</h3>
      <p>Modular weapon framework allowing swappable fire modes, attachments, and procedural recoil patterns.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Ability Framework</h3>
      <p>Data-driven ability system built on Unreal's Gameplay Ability System (GAS) with custom extensions.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>UI / UX</h3>
      <p>In-world diegetic UI elements alongside minimal HUD design to preserve immersion during gameplay.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="tech-card">
      <h3>Version Control</h3>
      <p>Git with LFS for asset management across the project lifecycle.</p>
    </div>

  </div>
</section>
